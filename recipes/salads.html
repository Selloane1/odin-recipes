Best Macaroni Salad

Very easy and delicious! I am constantly asked to bring this salad to parties and picnics. The cheese and ham are key additions to an old favorite.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 437 calories; protein 13.4g; carbohydrates 43.8g; fat 23g; cholesterol 28.4mg; sodium 358.8mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package macaroni
  • Â½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup cucumber - peeled, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, mayonnaise and mix well. Stir in cucumber, onion, ham and cheese. Mix well. Drizzle oil and vinegar over top and toss. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
